Culture of information sharing

January 19, 2016

All the living organisms on this planet including humans are consuming the information from their surroundings somehow and making choices suitable for their sustainable development and long life. Open and wide spread and reach of information is really important for any society and individual to develop. Making sense of some of infinite supply of information and doing something with it is what makes us Humans. It’s very much like food, what we eat has direct impact on our body.
